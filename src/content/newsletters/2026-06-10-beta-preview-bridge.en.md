Today we're sharing the mission our beta families loved most: **"Elephant Bridge."**

## What happens in 12 minutes

1. **It starts with Moongi's question** — "An elephant needs to cross the river. What should we do?"
2. **Choosing materials** — the child picks wood, stone, or rope with hand gestures
3. **Building the bridge** — the on-screen bridge takes shape from the child's choices
4. **The balance game** — while the elephant crosses, the child keeps balance with left/right controls
5. **Show and tell** — "Why is your bridge strong?" The child explains it out loud

Did you notice there's not a single watch-only step? Speaking, choosing, building, explaining — the child is the protagonist the whole way through.

## What beta families said

One father wrote: *"Even after closing the app, my kid kept talking about the bridge at the dinner table. That never once happened after YouTube."*

That's the sentence we most wanted to hear. Conversation that continues off-screen — that's the kind of learning MongleKids is built for.
