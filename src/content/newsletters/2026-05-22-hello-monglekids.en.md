Hello! We're the MongleKids team, and we're genuinely excited to send our very first issue.

## Why MongleKids?

AI already knows every answer. So we chose the opposite direction: **not an AI that hands out answers, but an AI friend that asks good questions.** When a child asks "why?", Moongi asks back — "what do *you* think?"

MongleKids is a creative-convergence learning service where children aged 4–9 learn through play by talking with AI characters. Building bridges, hunting colors, inventing stories — creativity, thinking, and expression grow along the way.

## What you'll find in this newsletter

- **Product news** — new missions, features, and behind-the-scenes from the beta
- **Creative learning ideas** — small activities you can try with your child tonight
- **Launch & early access** — beta invitations and launch dates, before anyone else

We won't email often. But when we do, it will be worth opening.

## Where we are today

We're polishing the first missions together with our beta families. Every time a child shouts "let's build the elephant bridge!" at Moongi, we remember exactly why we started.

See you in the next issue. 🎈

— The MongleKids team
